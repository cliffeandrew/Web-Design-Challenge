Plot One provides a visualization of Max Temperature by Latitude

Latitudes within +/-20 degrees of the equator generally have a Max temp of 60F to 85F

There are very few cities in the Southern Hemisphere below -50 Latitude

Virtually all Northern Hemisphere cities north of 40 Latitude have Max temps below 50F

Plot Two provides a visualization of City Latitude vs Humidity

All latitudes have a significant number of cities with max humidity at 100%

Virtually no cities within 20 degrees of the Equator have a max humidity under 20%

Most cities 45 degrees or more from the Equator have max humidity of greater than 60%

Plot Three provides a visualization of City Latitude vs Cloudiness

A large proportion of cities at all latitudes have Cloudiness at either 0% or between 75% and 87%

There are a gap in Cloudiness at and near 60% across all latitudes

Visually, latitude does not appear to play a significant role in Cloudiness%

Plot Four provides a visualization of City Latitude vs Wind Speed

No cities below the 44th Parallel in the chart have avg wind speed over 25 mph

Southern Hemisphere cities above -20 Latitude have avg wind speed below 15 mph

Roughly ten or fewer cities have avg wind speed of less than or equal to 1 mph
