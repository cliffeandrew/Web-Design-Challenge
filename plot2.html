Description

Plot Two provides a visualization of City Latitude vs Humidity


Ordered list

  1. All latitudes have a significant number of cities with max humidity at 100%
  2. Virtually no cities within 20 degrees of the Equator have a max humidity under 20%
  3. Most cities 45 degrees or more from the Equator have max humidity of greater than 60%
